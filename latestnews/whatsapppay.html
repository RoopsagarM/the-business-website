Whatspp Pay



WhatsApp Pay is the easiest way to buy groceries, etc by sending photos on the app. Users can then send and receive money on WhatsApp. Reliance and Facebook decided to invest 43,583crores to an E-Commerce platform JIOMART which is going to be a big competitor to Amazon and Flipkart. They have come up with an idea to connect the customers with the grocery stores through WhatsApp. AS Reliance JIO has 300 Million users from India which is largest Telecom company in India and WhatsApp is used by 388 Million users, so this would be the advantage for the JIOMART. This deal is second-largest investment of Facebook. The deal benefits Facebook via "partnership with the largest retail player in India, where partnership starts with grocery, and later could be extended to medicine distribution, fashion and lifestyle stores, food delivery, etc," Credit Suisse said in a report. WhatsApp Pay will be available for the users in India by month-end. Initially, the payment will be available for ICICI Bank, Axis Bank and HDFC Bank. However, the country's largest public sector bank, the State Bank of India (SBI), will be added at a later stage
