COMBAT SPORTS IN OLYMPICS:

Amateur boxing (1904-2016) has been staged at every summer Olympic games since 1904 except Stockholm in 1912 due to Swedish law.Judo (1964, 1972-2016) was not included in the 1968 Mexico City summer Olympics. Women's judo was added to the Olympics in 1992 in Barcelona. Taekwondo became an official medal sport at the 2000 Sydney Olympic Games.Wrestling Greco-Roman (1908-2016) is the first form of wrestling to be held at the Olympic Games.Wrestling Freestyle (1920-2016) was modified at the 2000 Sydney Games and reduced the amount of weight categories provided.Fencing (1896-2016) is one of the five activities which have been featured in every modern Olympic Games.
