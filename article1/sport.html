“TOP 5 SPORTSWEAR BRANDS”

  Sravika Kakarla



1. NIKE:




Nike is an American Multinational Company founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight.The company was renamed Nike, Inc., in 1978 .Their headquarters is in Oregon, U.S.Nike's mission statement is to “bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world”.The Company takes its name from the Greek goddess of victory and has a swoosh logo. ‘Just Do It’ is another trademark of Nike.Part of Nike’s success is owed to endorsements by such athletes as Michael Jordan, Mia Hamm, Roger Federer, and Tiger Woods. Nike remains the world’s most valuable apparel brand, valued at US$34.8 billion.Nike is the largest manufacturers of athletic shoes and apparel and also is a major player in sports equipment manufacturing. It is one of the most popular clothing brands and most revenue making manufacturers in the world.



2. ADIDAS:




Adidas is a well-known German multinational corporation and the second largest sportswear manufacturer in the world.The name Adidas (written “adidas” by the company) is an abbreviation of the name of founder Adolf (“Adi”) Dassler.Adidas products are traditionally marked with a three-stripe trademark, which remains an element in the company’s newer “trefoil” and “mountain” logos. Headquarters are in Herzogenaurach, Germany.From leggings, jackets, pants, hoodies, and t-shirts to its lucrative forays onto FIFA’s sacred fields, Adidas has made itself truly memorable.Some of big brands like Reebok, Runtastic, Matix are also subsidiaries of Adidas group.The current net worth of Adidas is $40 billion.Catering also to cricket, basketball, lacrosse, golf, tennis, gymnastics, skateboarding and running, the brand has spread its roots far and wide.



3)PUMA:




Puma journey began with two brothers with a talent for making sports performance enhancing shoes. Rudolf and Adolf Dassler founded Dassler Brothers Shoe Factory in 1924. Puma was founded by Rudolf Dassler in 1948 .They say that a cat has nine lives. Puma is one cat that needs no extra lives- the brand practically dominates in the Sports clothing and accessories industry.Puma is the third largest sportswear manufacturer in the world. Puma’s world famous jumping cat logo - introduced to the world in 1967- was the brainchild of a cartoonist from Nuremberg, Lutz Backes. This logo was amended in 1979 to a puma jumping across the corner of the word PUMA.The net worth of puma is $150 thousand dollars.



4. UNDER ARMOUR:




Under Armour was founded by Kevin Plank in 1996. At the time, he was a 23 year old special teams captain of the University of Maryland football team.Thе Аmеrісаn соmраnу соmрrіѕеѕ оf varied kіnd оf рrоduсtѕ like саѕuаl wеаr, fооtwеаr, аnd ѕроrtѕwеаr wіth а wіdе rаngе оf раttеrn аnd dеѕіgn fоllоwіng thе lаtеѕt trеnd. Тhе соmраnу іѕ ѕtаtіоnеd wіth іtѕ hеаdquаrtеr аt Маrу Lаnd іn thе Unіtеd Ѕtаtеѕ.Under Armour logo is a symbol of speed, strength, and athleticism.The American company ‘Under Armour’ has launched its first charging sports shoe, which got great response and appreciation across the world. The total estimated net worth of Under Armour is estimated to be about $3 billion.Тhіѕ еѕtееmеd Аmеrісаn соmраnу hаѕ bееn еѕtаblіѕhеd ѕuссеѕѕfullу wіth іtѕ glоbаl рrеѕеnсе.



5. NEW BALANCE:




New Balance is an American Sportswear manufacturer. Known for its premium and high quality shoes, New Balance is one of the biggest brands in the sportswear segment. It is ranked 5th as per the sales data. Based in Boston, Massachusetts, the multinational corporation was founded in 1906 as the New Balance Arch Support Company. In 1906, William J. Riley, an English immigrant, founded the New Balance company.Besides manufacturing men’s and women’s shoes for basketball, tennis, golf, hiking, running, and cross training, New Balance offers fitness apparel and shoes for kids and owns shoemaker Dunham.The net worth is in between $ 14.5K - $ 87.1K.New Balance has become one of the biggest sports brands out there as one of the top providers of sports apparel and equipment. It has a special line of products for sports enthusiasts interested in skateboarding.The company offers a variety of endorsements and sponsorships for sports teams and events, such as several European football clubs.
