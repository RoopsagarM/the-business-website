TOP 5 YOUNG ENTREPRENEURS IN INDIA

  Siri Manda



1. RITESH AGARWAL:




Ritesh Agarwal, the founder of OYO rooms, when he was just 18, he had thought to initiate an idea of a budget hotel chain featured with bedrooms and food. Very soon, he had discontinued his college to invigorate his start-up idea. Initially, he had started as Oravel Stays Pvt. Ltd. which became popular as OYO Rooms, a national sensation. Firstly, he started featuring 11 rooms in a Gurgaon Hotel, in 2013. Now, it ameliorated as 6,500 rooms across 170 Indian cities spanning over 5,500 properties. Being an ardent traveller, he came to know himself and understood the need for budget accommodation for Indians with standard facilities. He is one of the most influential Entrepreneurs in India.



2. INDRAJEET SINGH




Indrajeet Singh is the founder and CEO of iQUANTA, which is the largest CAT online coaching hub. Firstly, he started with Facebook as his platform to clear the doubts in a fraction of seconds. Later on, he started iQUANTA last year which gave splendid results in its very first year. His peer learning platform is mostly about unconventional methods and concepts which aren’t taught in conventional coaching centers. He made a brilliant start-up on students are not affording much on their further studies.




3. DEEPANJALI DALMIA:




Deepanjali Dalmia is a young woman of 26 age, who took the leap of faith by quitting her top dollar job in Ernst and Young in New York. She decided to work on women health in India and she came up with a start-up company called “HEYDAY CARES”. Many women are suffering to buy a sanitary pad in rural areas. She was astonished to initiate a company which produces organic sanitary pads made with bamboo fiber and corn with low costs.



4. RANVEER ALLAHBADIA:




Ranveer Allahbadia is an Indian social media community person. He takes care of his YouTube channel as well as his own company. His company called MONK-E is an influencer marketing and video production company, managing novel talents nationwide. He initiated his YouTube channel in 2013 named as Beer Biceps which was subscribed by 2.3 million people.



5. AAYUSHMAN SINHA:




Aayushman Sinha called as a young talent manager at the age of 25 who initiated PRESENT as a multi-genre management company. He praised as Young business leader for his campaign named as #TakeIndiatotheWorld in which he gained substantial fame and colossal popularity. In addition, he was awarded as “YOUNG ENTREPRENEUR” of the year in 2018.
