Top 5 Freelancing Websites in India



Freelancing is a self-employed person who offers services, often working on several jobs for various clients at one time. Freelancers usually make money on a per-task basis, charging hourly or daily rates for their work. Freelance work is usually for a short-term. These services are offered online through different websites. In those top 5 websites are:



What is Freelancing?


1.Upwork (Upwork.com):


Upwork is a great place to find more clients, and to run and grow your own business. Freedom to work on ideal projects. On Upwork, you run your own business and choose your own clients and projects. Just complete your profile and we'll highlight ideal jobs. As a freelancer, Upwork isn't that good unless you're just starting out. It's good to build some initial income and experience, but any entrepreneur worth their while will tell you to build your own business afterwards. Launch your own website, create social media accounts, and learn how to pitch real world companies.



2.Guru (Guru.com):


Guru.com is one of the oldest USA based marketplaces, where the employer can hire talented freelancers from various industrial domains to complete individual projects and jobs. It is a flexible Dashboard that provides employers and freelancers to connect with a platform to collaborate on different projects. Guru has a consumer rating of 1.46 stars from 98 reviews indicating that most consumers are generally dissatisfied with their purchases. Guru also ranks 73rd among Freelancing sites. The most common issues with Guru are around customer service, which is not as good as expected by some customers.





3.PeoplePerHour (PeoplePerHour.com):


People-Per-Hour gives you a unique way to discover services to grow your business. Explore fixed-price offers from as little as £10 — Giving you the freedom to test ideas cost effectively. Once you're happy with the results, request a follow-on project. Under this definition, no—People-Per-Hour is not a scam. It's a genuine freelancing platform that is not fraudulent and operates legally. It has legal terms & conditions, which are available to read on their website. It has its problems, but that doesn't mean it's a scam.



4.Speedlancer (speedlancer.com):


Speed lancer is a freelance platform that enables users to sell or purchase professionals skills in content creation, marketing, and design. Speed lancer is a freelance platform that enables users to sell or purchase professionals skills in content creation, marketing, and design. Speed lancer is the world's fastest freelance marketplace, delivering design, writing & data-entry work in just 4 hours by curated freelancers. They help start-up founders, digital agencies, SMBs, marketers and enterprises get tasks done quicker than ever before, with outstanding quality. In January 2015 they were accepted into 500 Start-ups Batch 12, and have relocated from Melbourne, Australia to San Francisco.



5.Fiverr (Fiverr.com):


Fiverr is an online marketplace for freelance services. Founded in 2010 and based in Tel Aviv, Israel, the company provides a platform for freelancers to offer services to customers worldwide. For example, buyers look for services within a lot of categories like music and audio, video and animation and others. Meanwhile, sellers are the people offering those services. So, they create Gigs that buyers pay for. Fiverr is not a scam. All its shortcomings aside, It's a legitimate freelancer website, where you get to progressively build your profile on your way to earning a decent income from home. As with any other crowded freelance marketplaces, you have to stand out.
