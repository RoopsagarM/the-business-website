BEST WEB SERIES TO WATCH



1)SACRED GAMES:


Sacred games is one of the best web series based on Vikram Chandra’s 2006 novel, directed by Vikramaditya Motwane and Anurag Kashyap.It depicts action,crime,and drama in a powerful way. The series stars Saif Ali Khan, Radhika Apte and Nawazuddin Siddiqui in the lead.The story mainly revolves around the Sartaj Singh(Saif Ali Khan), a Mumbai police officer who receives a phone call from gangster gangster named Gaitonde(Nawazuddin Siddiqui) who even thinks him to be the God ,tells him to save the city within 25 days. The first season follows Singh trying to uncover clues about Gaitonde's past while also learning of a connection between Gaitonde and his father.In season two, Gaitonde's story continues in flashbacks, which again affects things in the present for Sartaj.This police officer also makes every possible attempt to save Mumbai from any disaster after getting possible threats from the gangster.You find yourself to be traveling through a series of mystery, drama and action that makes you watch it.Isn’t it exciting to watch the drama ? So to know much about this interesting story and the bold steps taken by the sincere police officer everyone should watch it




2)STRANGER THINGS:


Stranger Things is a drama-horror web television series created by the Duffer Brothers and series premiered on Netflix on July 15, 2016.The series stars an ensemble cast including Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Finn Wolfhard, Millie Bobby Brown, Gaten Matarazzo and many other brilliant actors. This story begins in November 1983 with the disappearance of a young boy amid. When the family,friends and local police search for the disappeared boy they get to know an extraordinary mystery involving top-secret government experiments, terrifying supernatural forces and the discovery of one very strange little girl.And now the story is going to be interesting and revolves around the mystery and discovery of the girl with superpowers.Stranger Things has attracted record viewership on Netflix.The series has received multiple awards and nominations including 31 Primetime Emmy Award nominations (and six wins) etc.So to know more amazing contents,watch it and stay connected to it.




3)BREATHE:


Breathe is a crime series launched in January 2019.It mainly explores the themes of crime,deaths of innocent people. It premiered on Amazon Video on 26 January 2018.The series stars R. Madhavan, Amit Sadh, Hrishikesh Joshi, Sapna Pabbi in lead.The plot revolves around the lives of ordinary men who are faced with unexpected situations.Kabir Sawant, a talented officer of the crime division, assembles the missing pieces of unrelated deaths, all of which lead him to a common suspect. The twisty plotting, however,makes the biggest strength, which is quite unpredictable.It is exciting to watch how the drama unfolds between the suspect who is trying to save his dying son and the officer who is adamant to impart justice
