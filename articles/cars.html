LUXURIOUS CARS OWNED BY TOLLYWOOD ACTORS



1)PRABHAS:


South Rebel Star Prabhas is an ardent lover of luxury cars. The Rolls Royce Phantom a full-sized luxury car is one of the most prized assets owned by the Telugu super star Prabhas and which costs 8 crores is one of his prized possessions. This is not the only luxurious car the star owns. Prabhas owns BMW X3 which comes at a price of around ₹ 68 Lakhs. Prabhas is also the proud owner of a Jaguar XJR which costs around Rs 2.08 crore. He also owns a silky black Range Rover which is his regular ride. The Range Rover is also one of the most expensive SUVs as he bought it for nearly Rs 3 crore.




2)BALAKRISHNA:


Balakrishna who is popularly known as Nata Simha, passionate of cars. His brand-new car was BMW 7 series edition, which is nearly 1.5 crores, Bentley which is 3.5 crores and he also owns a Porsche Panamera (Rs.2.3 crore), which is only a 4-seater car in his luxurious cars collection.

Mega star Chiranjeevi has a huge collection of luxurious cars. As, he is an enthusiast of new cars, he owns a Range Rover Vogue, Toyota Land Cruiser. Recently he got a Rolls Royce Phantom black which costs 3 crores as a gift on his 59th birthday which was given to him by his son Ram Charan Tej.
