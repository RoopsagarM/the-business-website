IMPACT OF CORONA ON TRAVEL AND TOURISM SECTOR



Tourism plays a major role in different countries in which people in the society prefer traveling and tourism by the purpose of their work and enjoyment with their families. Due to the pandemic situation of all the countries which are affected by the Corona virus comparatively with other sectors travel and tourism sector had a great loss. A statement by World Travel & Tourism Council and The Oxford Economics, Travel and Tourism Investments expected to reach US$652.4 billion in 2011 and is forecast to increase to US$1.5 trillion by 2021.A sudden transformation in all the countries people are informed to stay in their home so that travel and tourism sector are facing a lot of problems. Due to the extinction of lockdown in different countries people are suggested not to travel any place. The corona virus epidemic kept nearly 50million jobs in global travel and tourism sector at risk. When the outbreak is over, it could take 10months for the industry to recover.



Around 8,50,000 people travel from Europe to United States but due to the present situation in 50 million jobs around 30 million of the jobs would be lost in Asia,7 million in Europe and 5 million in America and other countries. The tourism and travel sector accounts for 10% of the world’s GDP and reduction of jobs. Most of the airlines and the cruise ships which exports and imports different travelling goods are more affected than the hotels, restaurants and malls. In many of the countries the famous tourist places and so many places where people enjoy the most like beaches are now empty.

In weekends, normal days and holidays these places are full of crowd in which some people from other countries visit these places and some people travel from one place to other place due to their jobs. National parks are also affected a lot due to Corona because the wild creatures in are not given food as their no availability of people to keep food for the animals. As a part of lockdown some safety measures are suggested for people like social-distancing. People usually visit more tourist places and travel to different places. In these places people gather and enjoy, where lot of crowd gathers there would be spread of disease to people so that these tourist places are closed for some days as it neglects the social-distancing among people. Amusement parks and water parks which are mostly preferred by people for their enjoyment are affected and experiencing troubles and a great loss.
